What the simulator is doing

This simulator examines the outcome of a species distribution model (SDM) for kudzu that links its presence to climate conditions across the U.S.

The simulator blends between a present-day climate map and a future climate map. When the slider is near 0%, you are seeing a pattern closer to today’s climate. As you move the slider toward 100%, the pattern shifts toward a warmer, altered climate around ~2050.

The checkboxes control which climate variables the SDM is allowed to use. Your job is to:

  • move the climate slider,
  • turn variables on and off, and
  • look for patterns on the map that seem realistic (or unrealistic) for kudzu.

You don’t need the exact equations. Focus on the patterns and think about whether they make biological sense for a warm, moisture-loving vine.
